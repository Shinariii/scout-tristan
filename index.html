04 Scout Notes
Strength
Strong Early Laner

Averages positive gold diff at 10 on most champions. Jayce, Ambessa, Gnar, and Pantheon all show consistent early leads, indicating strong trading patterns and wave management.

Weakness
Lead Conversion

Jayce shows +326 gold at 10 but only 33% win rate across 12 games — a significant gap suggesting difficulty converting early advantages into game wins through mid/late macro.

Strength
Best Champion: Rumble

77.8% win rate across 9 games with positive gold diff. Most balanced performer — wins lane and wins games. Highest priority pick to enable or deny.

Note
Renekton Anomaly

Loses lane hard on Renekton (-570 gold at 10) but maintains 66.7% win rate. Suggests strong teamfighting or macro awareness that compensates for poor lane phases.

Methodology

Data pulled via Riot Games API. Last 50 ranked solo/duo games analyzed. Timeline data used for gold diff at 10. All stats normalized per minute where applicable to account for game length variance.
